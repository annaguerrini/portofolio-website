ABOUT ME

WHO AM I?

I’m Anna, a Portuguese–Italian UX/UI designer born and raised in Lisbon in the 90's. For as long as I can remember, I have been fascinated with the human mind and behavior. My background merges science, creativity, and technology, a mix that has allowed me to approach UX with a curious, analytical mind while embracing the creative side of design.


WHAT HAVE I BEEN DOING?

With 3 years of experience in the fintech field, I’m now working at Trading Central as a UX/UI Designer. I could give you the same generic list of tasks every designer puts on their portfolio (and yes, I talk to cross-functional teams pretty much every single day), but instead, here’s what I actually do:
- Untangle messy design problems and (try to) make them simple
- Push pixels until my eyes get dizzy
- Translate business needs into interfaces users actually enjoy (and vice-versa!)
- And occasionally, explain for the 100th time why “just adding a tooltip” isn’t the solution.
Believe it or not, all of this translates into some pretty cool metrics (see work sectionto find out more)


BEYOND WORK

I’m curious by nature and always chasing new things to learn: design, photography, books, AI or dancing until I run out of breath. Music is a big part of my day-to-day life and so are the Formula 1 race weekends.



WHY WORK WITH ME?

I’m resilient, adaptable, and endlessly motivated to make each project better than the last. I believe the best work happens in happy, collaborative environments where everyone gets a chance to grow.
